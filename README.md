## 🔥 What is EFU?

The **EFU (Energy Flux Unit)** is not another sustainability metric – 
it is a **biophysical audit framework** that measures civilizational 
metabolic integrity beyond GDP, ESG, and financial abstractions.

**1 EFU = 20 kg/day/person** (human metabolic reference)

This simple unit allows us to measure:
- Energy systems (104.8 Energy License)
- Water systems (104.6 Water Framework)
- Food systems (104.23 Agricultural License)
- Waste systems (600.x Antiflux Parasites)
- Regenerative alternatives (700.x Symbiotic Systems)

**Key Innovation:** The EFU measures **not just the state, but the 
velocity and acceleration** of civilizational change (104.60 CEWS – 
Civilization Early Warning System).
