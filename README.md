## 🔥 What is EFU?

The **EFU (Energy Flux Unit)** is not another sustainability metric – it is a **biophysical audit framework** that measures civilizational metabolic integrity beyond GDP, ESG, and financial abstractions.

**1 EFU = 20 kg/day/person** (human metabolic reference)

This simple unit allows us to measure:
- Energy systems (104.8 Energy License)
- Water systems (104.6 Water Framework)
- Food systems (104.23 Agricultural License)
- Waste systems (600.x Antiflux Parasites)
- Regenerative alternatives (700.x Symbiotic Systems)

**Key Innovation:** The EFU measures **not just the state, but the velocity and acceleration** of civilizational change (104.60 CEWS – Civilization Early Warning System).

## 🌟 Core Axioms (v5.1 "Diamond")

### **Axiom-0: Biophysical Homeostasis**
Civilizational sustainability is thermodynamically defined:



Ṡ_gen (entropy generation) ≤ Ṡ_reg (regeneration capacity)


If **EFU > 1.0** → parasitic mode (unsustainable).

### **Axiom-1: Matter-Energy Consistency**
There is no "bit without atom" – every digital, cognitive, or financial process has a **physical cost**.

### **Axiom-2: Irreversibility and Lock-in**
Some degradations are **practically irreversible**:

| Degradation | W_irrev | Time to Restore |

| CO₂ emission | 300/1000 | 100-1000 years |
| Soil degradation | 700/1000 | Centuries |
| Species extinction | 900/1000 | Never |
| PFAS contamination | 950/1000 | 1,000-10,000+ years |
